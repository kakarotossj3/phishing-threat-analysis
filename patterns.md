# Observed Threat Patterns

## Common Techniques

- Credential harvesting via fake login pages
- Use of urgency (e.g., "account will be suspended")
- Spoofed domains with slight variations

## Domain Patterns

- Newly registered domains (< 30 days)
- Typosquatting

## Language Patterns

- Poor grammar OR highly polished corporate language
- Impersonation of IT or banking institutions
